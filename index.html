Why you are interested

A banking & finance master's student who likes to build and make — small web tools, prototypes, and the occasional exhibited artwork.

Right now I'm doing my MSc in Banking and Finance at ZHAW in Zurich. Alongside the coursework I build little web projects to learn by doing — a QR code for a game prototype, a seeder site, and a daily-nudge app.

Away from the spreadsheets I make art and have shown work in exhibitions. I like the place where finance, code, and creativity overlap.
